Video and Audio Settings

Change the video and audio encoding settings below.

Framing

Mesh Radio Video Stream Encoding

This stream is used for video over the primary low-latency and high-bandwdith radio link. Typical bitrates are 1000-4000 kpbs. To disable the stream, select "Disabled" in the Bitrate drop down menu.

ATAK Stream Encoding

This stream is used for video to the TAK network. These streams are typically used on small screens. Typical bitrates are 500-1000 kpbs. To disable the stream, select "Disabled" in the Bitrate drop down menu.

Audio Stream Encoding

The audio encoding information can be changed below. Typical bitrate is 128 kbps. To disable the stream, select "Disabled" in the Bitrate drop down menu.
